Binagoongan Talong sa Gata
 
https://www.kawalingpinoy.com/binagoongan-talong/

Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 medium aubergines, cut into cubes
- 2 cm ginger, finely chopped
- 1 stick celery, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon garlic oil or 2 cloves garlic
- tofu sticks for 2 people
- 1 large tomato, chopped
- 1 tablespoon miso
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 0.2l coconut milk
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 2 mild chili peppers, stemmed and minced


Instructions

- Soak the tofu sticks in very hot water for 30 minutes, drain and chop.
- Cook the aubergines 7 minutes in the microwave at full power (900W).
- Cook tomato 2 minutes in the microwave at full power (900W).
- Heat the oil in a deep pan. When the oil is hot, add ginger, celery and garlic and fry fir a few minutes over medium heat. Add tofu sticks and cook until lightly browned.
- Add _miso_, tomatoes and vinegar and cook, stirring occasionally, for a few minutes.
- Add coconut milk, water, sugar and chili peppers and simmer for a few minutes over lower heat.
- Add the aubergines and continue to cook until liquid is reduced.  

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with rice
