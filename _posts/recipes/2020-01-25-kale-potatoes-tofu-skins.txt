200 g kale
400 g potatoes
60 g dried tofu skins
1 tablespoon sunflower seeds
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
2 dried tomatoes
1 tablespoons tomato puree
1/2 teaspoon tobanjan
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 clove garlic
1 cm ginger
sesame seed oil, rapeseed oil
1 tablespoon soy sauce

wash the kale and squeeze forcefully, repeat a few times
soak the dried tofu skins in boiling hot water for at least 20 mins
soak the dried tomatoes  in boiling hot water for at least 20 mins

Drain the tofu skins and cut into strips
Cut the potatoes in quite small chunks and cook them in the microwave for 10 minutes at 900W
Drain the cooking liquid into a small bowl


Fry the garlic, ginger, cumin and seeds for a few minutes over medium heat. 
Add the kale and fry over high heat until it just starts to wilt
Add the cut tofu skins to the pan and fry for a few minutes more.
Add the potatoes to the pan and fry for a few minutes more until the start to brown. 
Turn down the heat.

Add the tomato puree, tobanjan and soy sauce to the cooking liquid of the potatoes. Stir until it's smooth, then add to the pan and stir in.
